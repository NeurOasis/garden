How to Use These Icons

  1. Print these pages on card stock or heavy paper if possible.
  2. Cut out the icons along the dashed lines, using the corner marks as guides.
  3. Optional: Laminate the icons for increased durability.
  4. Add to your visual schedule by placing them in the "Activity" or "Options" column as needed.

Note: These indoor activity icons complement the outdoor activity icons from previous pages. Together they provide a complete set of visual supports for Zach's daily schedule.
